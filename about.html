Hello!
I'm Riste Gjorgjiev, a passionate hobby photographer based in Kavadarci, Macedonia. Ever since I picked up my first camera, I've been captivated by the power of photography to freeze time and tell compelling stories. What started as a casual interest has grown into a profound love for capturing the world around me.

My work spans various genres, from the serene beauty of nature to the dynamic energy of urban landscapes, and the intimate expressions found in candid portraits. I believe that every moment holds a unique story, and through my lens, I strive to highlight the extraordinary in the ordinary.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new places, hiking through scenic trails, or simply enjoying a good book. Photography is more than just a hobby for me; it's a way to connect with people, places, and moments in a deeply meaningful way. Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I hope my images inspire you and evoke the same sense of wonder and joy that I experience in creating them. Let's embark on this visual journey together!
